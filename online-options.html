Rayyan institute

Rayyan institute is a purely online education platform based in Canada with instructors from a variety of countries including the UK and US. The institute offers a range of courses on the following topics:

  • Quranic sciences (specifically looking at Tafseer of different Surahs)
  • Hadith sciences
  • Islamic belief and practice
  • Islamic History
  • Islamic spirituality
The classes are pre-recorded and last around 45 - 60 minutes.
Fees: Individual courses are not charged. Instead an access fee ($8.99/Month or $99/Year) for all courses needs to be paid via online payments. This will give you unlimited access to all the courses on the website.

Head over to https://rayyaninstitute.com/ for more information and don’t hesitate to contact them for further information if required.

Bayyinah

Bayyinah.tv is the online growing video collection of exclusive Quranic and Arabic videos delivered mainly by Ustadh Nouman Ali Khan, Shaykh Nassir Jangda and Shaykh Omar Suleiman. Series all aim to make the Quran and Arabic accessible in a simple and clear manner. These includes story nights, Quran in depth, story nights and thematic explorations of different surahs.

This platform will Insha’Allah enable you to gain a better understanding of the Quran and the many lessons we can derive from it, especially as young muslims.

Fees: $11/ Month or $112/year

The access program aims to provide an Arabic part time course available online and accessible to everyone. It is broken down into three levels that you can customise to suit your schedule.

Fees: $697 or 3 $232.33 instalments

Head over to http://bayyinah.com/about/ for more information and don’t hesitate to contact them for further information if required.

Cambridge Islamic College

Cambridge Islamic college provides both online and onsite diplomas. Dr Mohammad Akram Nadwi is the deen of the college and is a very prominent scholar with over 30 years of teaching experience. He is also a writer of books used all over the world in madrasahs and other institutes.

The college offers a variety of diplomas however the essential Islamic sciences (level 1- 3) and the Arabic and Islamic studies diplomas are options you recommend you look at.

Essential Islamic sciences

  • Term 1: Fundamentals of Islam (Usul al deen)
  • Term 2: Seerah of the Major prophets
  • Term 3: Essential Fiqh for modern times
Classes are pre-recorded and will last 3 hours. There will be one hour live tutorials fortnightly. (All lessons are taught by Shaykh Akram Nadwi).
Fees: 6 £99 instalments

Arabic and Islamic studies

This is a 2-year foundation/ Intermediate level course in Arabic and Islamic studies. The following books will be studied:

  • Year 1: Al Arabiyyah lil Nash'een, Stories of the prophets, principles of Arabic grammar and Principles of Arabic morphology
  • Year two: Will build upon what was learnt in year 2 using more advanced books.
Classes are pre-recorded and consist of 3hour lessons weekly and one 1-hour live tutorial (All lessons are taught by Shaykh Akram Nadwi).
Fees: 10 x £100 or 5 x £200

Head over to http://www.cambridgeislamiccollege.org/ for more information and don't hesitate to contact them for further information if required.
